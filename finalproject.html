WHAT IS POWDER COATING

POWDER COATING:-

Powder coating is a type of coating that is applied as a free-flowing, dry powder.

What is the concept of powder coating?

A dry finishing process created by an electric charge that causes a dry powder to fuse to the surface of the metal

SIX TYPES OF POWDER COATING

  • Super Durable Polyesters
  • Epoxy-Polyester Hybrids
  • Fluoropolymers
  1. Prepare the surface for powder coating
  2. Select your powder coating color, texture or finish
  3. Apply the powder using a spray gun to provide the charge
